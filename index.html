About Me

I'm a First-Class MEng Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate from the University of Aberdeen, with a focus on robotics, embedded systems, and control engineering. I’ve completed individual and collaborative projects spanning from AI-driven robotics simulations to precision mechatronic systems, including my thesis on open- and closed-loop control of a custom-designed origami-based 3DOF platform.

My research has led to a published paper on integral resonant control in nanopositioning systems, and my practical skills include Arduino programming, 3D modelling and printing, MATLAB/Simulink, and computer vision using OpenCV.

I’ve completed internships supported by the Carnegie Trust and contributed to sensorless control research with the University of Aberdeen. Outside the lab, I’m an avid sim racer, weightlifter, and Formula 1 fan who enjoys boardgames and deep technical challenges.
